ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE

We are Christ Ambassadors, disciples and representatives of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ in accortdance with the scriptures 2 Corinthians 5 : 20.
We are a fellowship of believers with a burning desire to fulfil the great commission handed down by our Master Jesus Christ in Matthew 28 : 18 - 20 and Mark 16 : 15 - 16.
We are members of St. Paul's Anglican Church, Oke-Afa, Isolo Archdeaconry, Diocese of Lagos West.

Our Mission

To reach the unreached in all parts of the world with the good news of Jesus Christ.

To impact the younger generations for Christ and to raise more labourers in the mission field.

To work towards the expansion of the kingdom of God in this world and bring salvation and hope to lost and dying souls.

To empower Christians to stand boldly in the faith and live as shinning light unto the world.

Our Vision

To reconcile man to his Maker, the Lord God Almighty.
To make disciples of nations through evangelism i.e propagating the gospel of Jesus Christ to every nook and cranny of the world.

What We Do

We support our local church to carry out revival programs and crusades.

We hold meetings (fellowship, bible study, night vigils and retreat, seminar/conferences) to train and prepare our members for the work of the commission.

We carry our evangelism and missionary work within Lagos, different states in Nigeria and West Coast of Africa.

Membership

Membership is open to every Christian, especially to members of St. Paul's Anglican Church, Oke-Afa, Isolo. An intending member must be born again or aspiring to be born again as stated in John 3 : 3.

Members are expected to aspire through the grace of God, to shun all works of the flesh and show forth in their everyday lives, the fruit of the Holy Spirit as stated in Galatians 5 : 22 - 23.
